Beef Chao Fan

Description:

Beef Chao Fan is a Chinese inspired Filipino Fried Rice recipe that is mixed with ground beef, chopped vegetables, onion & garlic, and it seasoned with soy and oyster sauce. This rice dish is an inspiration from a popular Filipino fast-food restaurant Chowking.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cup of cooked white rice
  • 250 grams of ground beef
  • 1 cup of red onion chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic chopped
  • 2.5 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • ½ cup of chopped carrot
  • ½ cup of corn kernel
  • 2 cup of cabbage chopped
  • ½ cup of spring onion stalk chopped
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 tablespoon of canola oil 2 for scrambled egg and 2 for the fried rice
  • Salt and black pepper for taste

Instructions:

    Cooking Scrambled Egg

  1. Add 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil to the pan in medium heat.
  2. Crack the egg into a small bowl, and add salt & black pepper. Stir to mix
  3. Once the oil is hot, pour the egg into the pan. Stir gently till the scrambled egg is cooked.
  4. Use a fork or a wooden spoon to cut into smaller pieces. Set aside.

    5. Making the Fried Rice

  5. Add vegetable oil to a large pan. Once hot, add chopped garlic. Stir for 30 seconds and then add the chopped red onion. Stir again till it turns translucent.
  6. Add the ground beef. Stir again and let it cook till the juices are almost evaporated.
  7. Add the soy sauce and oyster sauce and continue stirring for 15 seconds
  8. Add the chopped cabbages, carrots, and corn kernel. Give a quick stir.
  9. Add the cooked leftover white rice, Gently stir to mix.
  10. Transfer the scrambled egg and chopped spring onion stalk. Mix to stir for one more time before turning off the heat.
  11. Serve with lumpia, siomai, or chopsuey.
